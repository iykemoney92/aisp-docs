## FAQs

### Do credits expire?

No! Credits never expire. Use them whenever you're ready at your own pace.

### Is chat really free?

Yes! **All chat messages are 100% free** regardless of which AI model you use. There's no credit cost for conversations with GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, or any other chat model.

### What happens if I run out of credits mid-generation?

If you start a generation with sufficient credits, it will complete even if your balance reaches zero. You'll need to add credits before starting new paid generations.

### Do subscribers need credits?

Subscribers get FREE image, video, and sound generation (unlimited), but they still need credits for upscaling operations. Subscribers also receive bonus credits with their plan (250-12,000 depending on plan) and get 10-20% bonus on credit purchases.

### Can I get a refund?

Refund policies are handled through RevenueCat and the platform where you made the purchase (App Store, Google Play, or web). Contact support for refund requests.

### Are there hidden fees?

Absolutely not. The credit costs shown are exactly what you pay. No hidden fees, no setup costs, no monthly fees (unless you subscribe).

### How do I earn free credits?

Multiple ways:
- **30 credits** signup bonus
- **2 credits** per ad watched
- **30 credits** per referral (both you and your friend)
- Daily check-in rewards
- 3-day trial with free generations

### Can I transfer credits to another account?

No, credits are non-transferable between accounts. Each account maintains its own credit balance.

### What's the difference between credits and subscriptions?

- **Credits** = Pay-per-use for generations (images, video, sound, upscales)
- **Subscriptions** = Unlimited generations + bonus credits (except upscales which always cost credits)
- **Chat** = Always FREE for everyone

### Do I need a subscription?

No! You can use AI Studio Plus with just credits. Subscriptions are optional and provide unlimited generations (except upscales) plus bonus credits.

### What's the best value?

- For occasional users: Buy credit packages as needed
- For regular users: **Monthly subscription** ($15/month)
- For power users: **Yearly subscription** ($150/year) - saves $30
- For maximum credits: **Mega Pack** with subscriber bonus

### Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime through your device's subscription management (App Store or Google Play) or through RevenueCat on web.
