# Quick Start - Your First AI Creation

Ready to make something amazing? Let's get you creating with AI in the next 5 minutes. No experience needed!

## Step 1: Jump In (2 minutes)

### Create Your Account

1. Head to [AI Studio Plus](https://studio.aisp.app)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Choose your way in:
   - **Google** - One click and you're in
   - **Apple** - Fast and secure
   - **Email** - Traditional but reliable

That's it! You're now part of the AI Studio Plus community.

:::tip Pro Tip
Google and Apple sign-in are fastest - no email verification needed. You'll be creating in seconds!
:::

## Step 2: Get Your Creative Credits (1 minute)

Think of credits as your creative fuel. You use them to create with AI.

**Starting Out?**
- You get **10 free credits** to explore
- That's enough for 5-10 images or a nice chat session
- Try everything before buying more

**Ready for More?**

You have 3 ways to get credits:

1. **Buy Credits** - Pay once, use anytime
   - Start small: $10 = 100 credits
   - Credits never expire
   - Top up whenever you want

2. **Subscribe** - Get credits monthly at a discount
   - Best value for regular creators
   - Cancel anytime
   - Unused credits roll over

3. **Watch Ads** - Earn credits for free
   - Watch short videos
   - Get credits instantly
   - No payment needed

Pick what works for you!

## Step 3: Create Something (2 minutes)

Now for the fun part! Pick your adventure:

### Adventure 1: Have Your First AI Chat

**Perfect if you want to:**
- Get ideas for a project
- Write something fun
- Research a topic
- Just explore what AI can do

**How to do it:**
1. Click **Chat** in the sidebar (can't miss it)
2. You'll see a chat interface - just like texting
3. Try one of these to start:

```
"Help me come up with 5 unique birthday gift ideas for my mom who loves gardening"

"Write a funny short story about a cat who thinks it's a dog"

"I want to learn photography. Where should I start?"

"Create 3 Instagram captions for my beach vacation photo"
```

4. Hit **Enter** and watch the magic happen
5. Keep chatting! Ask follow-ups, refine ideas, go deeper

:::tip What it Costs
Most chat messages use 0.2-0.5 credits. Your 10 free credits = plenty of conversation!
:::

### Adventure 2: Generate Your First Image

**Perfect if you want to:**
- Create art for a project
- Make a meme
- Design something unique
- See your imagination come to life

**How to do it:**
1. Click **Images** in the sidebar
2. You'll see a text box - describe what you want
3. Try something like:

```
"A cozy coffee shop on a rainy day, warm lighting, illustrated style"

"Cute cartoon cat wearing sunglasses and a leather jacket"

"Sunset over mountains, vibrant colors, digital art"

"My portrait as a superhero, action pose, comic book style"
```

4. Pick your style (try "Realistic" or "Artistic" to start)
5. Click **Generate**
6. Wait 10-20 seconds and... boom! Your creation appears

**Love it?** Download it! **Want changes?** Adjust your description and try again.

:::tip What it Costs
Images use 0.5-2 credits depending on the model. Start with Stable Diffusion XL (cheapest) to experiment.
:::

### Adventure 3: Discover an AI Agent

**Perfect if you want to:**
- Get daily motivation
- Plan your meals
- Have a helpful AI companion
- See what the community created

**How to do it:**
1. Click **Agents** in the sidebar
2. Browse the **Marketplace**
3. Try one of these popular agents:
   - **Daily Motivation AI** - Start each day inspired
   - **Meal Planner** - Never wonder what's for dinner
   - **Soul Sync** - Get zodiac insights with AI
   - **Mental Therapy Helper** - A supportive listener

4. Click an agent to see what it does
5. Click **Start Chat** to try it out
6. See if it becomes your new favorite AI companion

:::info Free to Try
Using agents costs the same as regular chat - just 0.2-0.5 credits per message. Try as many as you want!
:::

## Common Questions

**Q: I used all my free credits. Now what?**
No pressure! Buy more when you're ready. Just $10 gives you 100 more credits to keep creating.

**Q: My image/video doesn't look right**
Totally normal! AI takes practice. Try:
- Describing it differently
- Using different words
- Checking the Prompt Library for ideas

**Q: Can I create multiple things at once?**
Yes! Open multiple tabs or switch between features. Create an image while chatting, plan a video while waiting - multitask away!

**Q: Where do my creations go?**
- Chats: Saved automatically in Chat history
- Images/Videos: Saved in your gallery
- Everything: Can be organized into Projects

**Q: Can I share what I make?**
Absolutely! Share to the Community, download and post elsewhere, or keep it private - your choice.
